What I have read about Seatrium

As Seatrium is the sponsor-company of the scholarship I am applying for, I have taken sometime to do some research about Seatrium and what excites me the most about Seatrium. Here are my findings:

- Seatrium is formed after the merger of Sembcorp Marine and Keppel Offshore and Marine
- Formed in 2023
- Seatrium has many subsidaries including Dolphin Shipping company Pte Ltd, Gravifloat AS and Jurong Shipyard Private Limited.
- Recently awarded a contract from SBM Offshore for FPSO Jaguar.

FPSO Jaguar

The news about the construction and installation of FPSO Jaguar has really excited me. Based on what I have read FPSO Jaguar:

- Design to produce 250,000 barrels of oil per day
- Gas treatment of 540 million cubic ft per day
- Water injection capacity of 300,000 barrels of water per day
- Will be deployed to Starbroek Bloack in French Guyana
