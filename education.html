Education

I graduated with a Diploma in Mechatronics and Robotics from School of Mechanical and Aeronautical Engineering, Singapore Polytechnic. My interest in engineering started since childhood as I was inspired by my father who was a former chief engineer. Being a Mechatronics student I studied various aspects of engineering such as mechanical, electrical and software engineering.

I graduated with good results. My final 3 semester GPAs were as follows;

4th Sem: 3.62
5th Sem: 3.69
6th Sem: 3.76.

I also earned a Distinction grade for my Final-Year Project(FYP) as well as a Gold Medal for the MAE FYP Exhibition. I was the overall group leader as well as the mechanical in-charge of the project.

I was also specially awarded the Certificate in Design and Innovation by SP for undertaking 3 project-based electives across 3 semesters namely;

- Digital Fabrication and Prototyping - Further Digital Fabrication and Prototyping - Transdisciplinary Innovation Project

I also held several leadership positions while studying in SP. I was the President of SP Water Polo where I led the team during COVID period. I was also the Vice-Captain of SP International Chess Club.

Prior to joining SP, I studied in Victoria School where I took O Levels and represented the school in soccer.
